Jude Kharchou is a Chechen-Syrian artist working with moving image and performance art to explore cultural memory, displacement, and connection to land. Their practice moves between digital and physical spaces, using film, video installation, stop-motion animation, creative coding, and print-based processes to document and reimagine histories and cultural practices. Through ephemeral and lasting forms of storytelling, they consider the body as both an archive and a site of grief, memory, and continuity.
